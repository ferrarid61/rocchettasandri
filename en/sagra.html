Festival of Saint Eurosia

The most traditional summer festival of the Modenese Apennines — Music, gastronomy and religious celebrations

A Festival Deeply Rooted in Tradition

The Festival of Saint Eurosia (Sagra di Santa Eurosia) at Rocchetta Sandri is one of the most traditional and beloved summer events of the Modenese Apennines. Celebrated as the patronal feast, it combines local religious devotion with good mountain cuisine and fun for all ages.

Traditionally held over the first weekend of July, it transforms the sports field of Rocchetta Sandri into a gathering place where the community comes together to celebrate its cultural and gastronomic heritage. The event is the beating heart of social life in this small Apennine village.

"The Festival of Saint Eurosia is not just a party: it is the living memory of the community, a memory passed down from generation to generation, celebrated with food, music and faith."

The Food Stand: The Heart of the Festival

The highlight of the event is the food stand, where visitors can taste the typical local specialties. Crescentine, borlenghi, fried dough (gnocco fritto) and polenta represent the traditional dishes of the Modenese Apennines, accompanied by grilled meats.

The event features special evenings dedicated to grilled Florentine steak, where the quality of the meat and the Apennine culinary tradition meet in the most authentic way. Everything is paired with unlimited wine and beer, for a complete gastronomic experience.

These dishes are not simply food: they are the tangible expression of the agricultural and pastoral culture of Rocchetta Sandri. Each preparation requires skill and dedication, the legacy of a culinary wisdom handed down over the centuries.

Entertainment Every Evening

Every evening of the festival comes alive with live music, traditional liscio orchestras that recall the dances of days gone by, and DJ sets for young people who love contemporary rhythms.

The night-time events include cocktail parties that go on late into the night, creating an atmosphere of festivity and conviviality. It is an occasion to dance, laugh and spend time together, transforming the small hamlet into a gathering place where generations mingle around music and tradition.

An Experience for the Whole Family

👶 For Children

Games and rides dedicated to the little ones are always available, such as inflatables and other attractions that make the festival an event accessible to all age groups.

⛪ Religious Celebrations

As it is the patronal feast, Sunday features solemn services with Holy Mass at the parish church and the recitation of the Holy Rosary in memory of the patron saint.

Saint Eurosia: Patron of the Village

Saint Eurosia is the patron saint of Rocchetta Sandri. The choice of this saint as patroness of the village is not accidental: Eurosia was a saintly abbess who embodies virtues such as generosity, dedication and protection of the community.

The festival is an opportunity to celebrate not only the saint, but also the identity of the village itself, its historical and cultural roots. It is an expression of popular faith rooted in medieval history and still alive in the hearts of the people of Rocchetta.

An Event Not to Be Missed

The Festival of Saint Eurosia represents the high point of the Apennine summer. It is the perfect occasion to discover Rocchetta Sandri, taste true mountain cuisine, dance to the rhythm of traditional music and meet a community that has preserved its traditions over the centuries.

Whether you are a food lover, an enthusiast of traditional music, or simply in search of authenticity, the Festival of Saint Eurosia will make you feel part of a story rooted in the centuries, celebrated each year as if it were the very first time.
